Our History

It all began on a chilly autumn afternoon in Stockholm, long before The Burger Joint became the beloved meeting place it is today. Hampus “Hampi” and Olle, two lifelong friends with a shared love for food and creativity, sat cramped inside a tiny student apartment kitchen surrounded by half-burnt burger buns, sizzling pans, and the unmistakable smell of determination. Both were passionate home cooks, constantly experimenting, competing, and pushing each other toward perfecting the ultimate burger. What started as friendly rivalry between two friends slowly grew into something bigger—an idea neither of them could ignore.

During their university years, Hampi studied entrepreneurship while Olle focused on culinary arts. Their evenings were spent combining their knowledge: Hampi sketched business plans on napkins while Olle crafted sauces from scratch and tested different blends of Swedish beef. Friends who visited constantly asked the same question: “Varför öppnar ni inte en restaurang?” The seed was planted. Still, the dream felt too big, too far away—at least until the summer of 2022, when they traveled together through Europe tasting street-food, burger joints, and local specialties from Berlin to Barcelona. Inspiration hit them hard. They returned home with notebooks full of flavors, ideas, and the confidence they had been missing.

Back in Stockholm, they rented a tiny basement kitchen—barely big enough for two people—and began what they jokingly called “the burger laboratory.” They tested hundreds of combinations, from brioche buns to toasted rye, from smoked mayo to truffle-infused ketchup. Some experiments were disasters, others brilliant breakthroughs, but every failure pushed them closer to perfection. After months of trial and error, they finally crafted what would become The Burger Joint’s signature creation: a juicy smash burger layered with caramelized onions, double cheese and a secret sauce that balanced smokiness, sweetness, and a hint of heat. That was the moment they realized: *We’re ready.*

But opening a restaurant is never easy. They spent nearly every penny they had saved, built furniture themselves from reclaimed wood, and painted the walls during late nights after work shifts. Friends and family helped whenever they could, offering tools, hands, and encouragement. When the doors finally opened in early 2023, they had no idea what to expect. Yet on the very first evening, something magical happened: a crowd formed. People who tried the burgers spread the word quickly—“Har ni testat de nya burgarna på Söder?” Within weeks, the little restaurant was packed every night, full of laughter, sizzling sounds, and the aroma that would soon become iconic.

Over time, The Burger Joint became more than a simple eatery. It became a place where students celebrated exam results, couples shared first dates, friends reunited, and families found a new favorite hangout. Hampi and Olle never forgot the promise they made in that tiny kitchen: that every burger served should be crafted with care, passion and genuine joy. Today, their dream continues to grow, and every customer who walks through the door becomes part of the story—one delicious bite at a time.

Our Vision

At The Burger Joint, our vision reaches far beyond serving delicious food. We aim to create a warm, authentic and welcoming environment where every guest feels at home. Our belief is simple: food tastes better when it brings people together. Whether you’re grabbing a quick lunch, sharing a dinner with friends, or treating yourself after a long day, we strive to make every visit memorable—one that blends great flavor with comfort, atmosphere and genuine hospitality.

We envision a future where sustainable cooking, quality ingredients, and honest craftsmanship stand at the heart of Swedish fast-casual dining. Every part of our menu is designed with intention. From locally sourced beef to hand-picked vegetables and thoughtfully crafted sauces, we choose ingredients that respect both flavor and the environment. We want our guests to not only enjoy what they eat but to feel confident about where it comes from. By supporting local suppliers and minimizing food waste, we work actively toward a cleaner and more responsible culinary future.

Our long-term dream is to share the spirit of The Burger Joint with more communities across Sweden—and eventually beyond its borders. But no matter how much we grow, we will always hold onto the values that started it all: passion, quality, creativity, and the belief that great burgers can change the ordinary into something extraordinary. As we look forward, we aim to keep innovating, surprising and delighting every person who walks through our doors. Because at the end of the day, our vision is not just about food—it’s about creating a place where stories begin, friendships form, and memories are made.
